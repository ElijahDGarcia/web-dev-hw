What am I all about?

My name is Elijah Garcia. I'm an aspiring artist, 3D designer, environmental designer, animator, content creator... Well, I'm an aspiring lot of things!

I was born in Montana, in the small town of Glasgow! It was discovered that my hometown was the most rural town in the entire US! That's pretty interesting, huh? I loved living there, but the bad internet access and need to educate myself drew me to Missoula, where I live now.

I love cats, dogs, and pretty much every animal! I grew up helping around the farm a lot, so I know my way around a horse or two. My family all work blue-collar jobs, and I'm hoping to be the first in my family to get a college degree!
